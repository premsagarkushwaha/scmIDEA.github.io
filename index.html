Supply chain event prediction and visibility

Supply chain management (SCM), which comprises all procedures that convert raw materials into finished commodities, is the central administration of the movement of goods and services.

The five most critical elements of SCM are strategy, sourcing raw materials, production, distribution, and feedback.

Problem Statement

Typically, SCM attempts to centrally control or link the production, shipment, and distribution of a product. By managing the supply chain, companies can cut excess costs and deliver products to the consumer faster. This is done by keeping tighter control of internal inventories, internal production, distribution, sales, and the inventories of company vendors.

Customer Attention

Every supplier should have a simple mechanism for making their items available. For simple processes, the majority of customers search for services online. In that situation, a web application can be crucial in grabbing the customer's attention. It will make it easier for customers to manage, track, and place orders. Customer and supplier relationships will improve as a result.

Production Management

A manufacturing company was experiencing problems with customer demand management, lengthy lead times brought on by a number of variables. The Company is considering a web-based application to address the current problems and automate the entire process. Which must be capable of automating the process using machine learning techniques and should have a dashboard that can assist in managing and studying the consumer using visualization approaches.

Distribution

In order to guarantee prompt, secure, and economical product delivery, the supplier must have strong logistics skills and distribution networks. To show customers how their orders were delivered, they should have access to sufficient data and media. It raises the supplier's perception of reliability.


Our Approach

So, we are come with the idea of web application where retailer and supplier can interact and can make supply chain process easier.

1. Dashboard and Analysis

According to our idea, a Supplier will have visualization of products demand by their customer(retailer) in his dashboard, all information by region, customer behavior, and also can manage product demand fulfillment requests.

2. Manufacturing Information

Supplier can easily check what are the material available in organization, and this information will help to predict expected delivery of the product to the retailer.

3. Demand Prediction and Automation

We will use machine learning algorithm to predict the future demand of the product. So, that they can start manufacturing to avoid supply bottleneck. It will enhance the productivity during the demand.Apply automation to start manufacture most demanded or coming demanded product by analyzing the previous data.



Component of Web-Application

This app will contain two parts, 1. Supplier Dashboard 2. Retailer Dashboard. First customer need to create a retailer account and then they can sign in into retailer dashboard where they can interact with the supplier. Here Organization is admin and will manage all the database of the retailer and other data.

Supplier dashboard

In organization dashboard supplier can view all the information of their product, availability of their product, demand of products, retailer products requests, can approve requests further, and can track shipment of the product send to the retailer etc. Following are dashboard features:-

  • Product demand prediction
  • Reatailer Request Management
  • Products demand visualization
  • Manufacturing Material Availability

Retailer Dashboard

In retailer dashboard, they can place an order of different products and after process of the request they can make payment. Also, they can customize the products by choosing available customization.


Workflow : steps

Log In

Retailer Sign up or Log in

Initially Customer need to Login in the Supplier web site. If customer does not have account then he need to make one account.

Request

Retailer Dashboard and Request

After login using credential he will be redirected to the retailer dashboard. Customer need to choose the require product and need to place an order. A request will be created to the supplier, customer need to wait for supplier approval and expected delivery.

Planning

Supplier Dashboard & planning

Here supplier can control all the request and according to availability of the product he can approve the customer request with expected delivery. He can visualize other data of the customers.

Product

Supplier Production request

After confirming the request supplier will send details of the requested product to the production area to start production of the product.

Testing

Quality test after Production

Following the development phase, the product will be sent for testing. A web application will be used to track every step.

shipment

Product Distribution and shipment

After the testing Phase supplier will approve the product for shipment.
